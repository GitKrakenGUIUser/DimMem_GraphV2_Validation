Click on your profile icon, then select Connect Hardware Wallet. Select Ledger, then click on Connect. An Open Ledger Live? window appears.Click Open Ledger Live.Once Ledger Live opens, an Open Device Bridge window appears. You are asked to allow a Websocket connection to your MetaMask account. LinuxThe workaround implemented by MetaMask doesn’t work for users on Linux. BODY: If you lost or forgot your Ledger Live password, you can reset your password by resetting Ledger Live. Resetting Ledger Live erases all settings and accounts on your computer. Once you have reset Ledger Live, you can then choose a new password and add your accounts back with your Ledger device. NOTE: By resetting Ledger Live, you will not lose your crypto assets since your private keys giving access to your funds remain secure on your Ledger device and are backed up by your recovery phrase.Resetting a lost or forgotten passwordOn the Welcome back page, click on I lost my password. After reading the warning, click on the Reset button. On the Welcome to Ledger page, click on the Get Started button.Click Enter Ledger app after ticking the boxes if you agree with Ledger’s Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.Select your device. Scroll down to the Already have a recovery phrase? section, then click on the Connect device option. On the Genuine check page, click on the Check my Nano button to verify that your device is genuine.Once it’s confirmed that your device is genuine, click on Continue.In Ledger Live, go to Settings, then click on the Password lock toggle ( ). Set up a new password. For step-by-step instructions on how to set up a password, refer to the following article: Set up password lock. Add your accounts back. For more information on how to add your accounts, refer to the following article: Add your accounts.
