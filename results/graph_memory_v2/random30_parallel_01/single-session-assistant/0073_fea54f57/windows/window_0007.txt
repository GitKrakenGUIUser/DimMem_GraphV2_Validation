[2023-05-24T04:17:00.500000, Wed] 10.User: I recently wrote this recipe with the help of ai: Nutrient-Rich Hearty Stew

Serves 6-8

Ingredients:

1 lb skinless, boneless chicken breast, diced (or substitute with turkey or salmon)
1 cup cooked lentils (or canned, drained, and rinsed)
1 cup chopped kale
1 cup chopped spinach
1 cup chopped broccoli
1 cup diced carrots
1 cup diced bell peppers (a mix of colors)
1 cup chopped tomatoes
1 large onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup cooked quinoa (or substitute with brown rice or barley)
1/4 cup chopped walnuts (or substitute with almonds)
1/4 cup raisins or chopped dried apricots
1/2 cup frozen mixed berries (e.g., blueberries, raspberries, or strawberries)
1 cup low-fat yogurt (or plant-based yogurt alternative)
3 cups low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth
1 cup fortified plant-based milk or dairy milk
2 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil
1 tbsp nutritional yeast
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
1/2 tsp ground ginger
Salt and pepper, to taste
Fresh herbs: chopped parsley, cilantro, or basil (optional)
Ground seaweed: kelp or nori (optional)
Instructions:

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil or avocado oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and garlic, and cook until softened and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
Add the diced chicken (or turkey or salmon) to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
Stir in the kale, spinach, broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, and tomatoes. Cook for about 5 minutes, allowing the vegetables to soften slightly.
Add the cooked lentils, quinoa, walnuts, raisins or chopped dried apricots, and mixed berries to the pot.
Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and fortified milk, stirring to combine. Add the ground turmeric, ground ginger, salt, and pepper. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have melded together.
Stir in the yogurt (or plant-based yogurt alternative) and nutritional yeast until well combined. Adjust the seasonings with salt and pepper to taste.
If using, sprinkle in ground seaweed (kelp or nori) for an extra iodine boost and mix well.
Garnish the stew with chopped fresh herbs like parsley, cilantro, or basil before serving.
Enjoy this nutrient-rich hearty stew as a part of a balanced diet, and feel free to customize the ingredients to suit your preferences or dietary needs.
